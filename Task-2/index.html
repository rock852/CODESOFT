About Us

BookWorld has been a leading platform in the book industry for over 10 years. We provide a wide range of services to book enthusiasts including book recommendations, author interviews, personalized book suggestions, and an online book store.

Our mission is to connect readers with the books they'll love and to support authors in sharing their stories with the world.

Current Goals: To expand our online store, increase our collection of books, and enhance user experience.

Future Goals: To become the go-to platform for book lovers globally, offering not just books, but also a vibrant community of readers and writers.

Book of the Previous Month

Title: Atomic Habits

Author: James Clear

Main Characters: This book does not have characters in the traditional sense, but includes real-life examples and case studies.

Story Description: "Atomic Habits" offers a comprehensive and practical guide to breaking bad behaviors and adopting good ones. James Clear draws on scientific research and real-life examples to explain how tiny changes can lead to remarkable results. Book of the Year

Title: Harry Potter and Chamber of Secrets

Author: J.K. Rowling

Main Characters:

Harry Potter

Hermione Granger

Ron Weasley

Albus Dumbledore

Severus Snape

Voldemort

Story Description: "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" is the first book in the Harry Potter series. It follows the story of Harry Potter, an eleven-year-old boy who discovers that he is a wizard and receives an invitation to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. At Hogwarts, Harry makes new friends and uncovers the truth about his parents' death and his connection to the dark wizard Voldemort. The book is a magical journey filled with adventure, friendship, and the battle between good and evil.
